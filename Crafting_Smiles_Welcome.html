Welcome.

If you’ve wondered whether you belong here — yes.

This page is your starting point: who can contribute, what counts, the seven pathways, and how to submit your work. No class required, no graduation date required.

Who Belongs Here

Four kinds of people contribute through the Hub.

The Crafting Smiles Hub exists for anyone who wants to make something for a child who needs a smile. It doesn’t ask whether you’re enrolled at Kellam or what year you graduated — it asks whether you have something to give.

Current Students

Any student at Kellam High School, in any grade, regardless of class enrollment.

Alumni

Former students who graduated and want to keep contributing — distance and time don’t matter.

Former STE(A)M Campers

Anyone who attended the summer STE(A)M Camps. Once a camper, always part of the project.

Friends of the Project

Parents, educators, makers, and anyone whose hands and hearts want to help.

The Three Non-Negotiables

Three things every contribution shares.

No matter which pathway you choose — these are the floor.

1
Made by you.

Original work — your hands, your design, your voice. Not someone else’s design, not commercially produced, not lifted from elsewhere. The making is what makes it count.

2
Connected to a OneWord.

Pick one of the 25 shared OneWords and let it shape your contribution. The word is the thread that ties every Smile Bag to the same project.

3
For a child who needs a smile.

Recipient-blind, age-appropriate, kindness-centered. You don’t know who gets it. They don’t know who made it. The kindness travels in both directions.

How to Submit

Each pathway has its own intake.

Most paths submit themselves through their kiosks. The Maker Path runs through Laura Howell directly — keep reading.

Bookmark
Spinner
Submit through the Crafting Smiles Kiosk’s built-in flow. Choose your OneWord, design your piece, and follow the on-screen submission steps.
Coloring Books
No formal submission. Pick a page from either ABC’s book, print on standard paper, and place it directly in a Smile Bag during assembly.
SNHS Bag Builder
Sign up through the SNHS Overview, follow the SNHS Student Guide, and submit your completed 10-item bag through the SNHS workflow.
Service Points
Build your 10-item bag, then submit through the Service Points Guide & Submission form. Your club sponsor signs off and points are awarded.
Maker Path
(Original 3D Designs)
For alumni, former campers, and friends with their own design tools:
  1. Choose your OneWord from the list of 25.
  2. Design your piece in your tool of choice — Tinkercad, Fusion 360, OnShape, Blender, or anything else you use.
  3. Share the design link with Laura Howell (text or email) for a quick alignment check before you print.
  4. Print on your printer, in the project palette where applicable.
  5. Coordinate logistics with Laura — ship, drop off, or hand-deliver depending on your distance from Kellam.

Every contribution gets a quick alignment check. If something needs a tweak, you’ll hear about it. No contribution is rejected without conversation — the kindness is what matters, and there’s almost always a way to land it.
