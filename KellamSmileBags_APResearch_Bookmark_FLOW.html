🔬 Did You Know?
Water never stops moving. Even when a river looks still, water flows underground, through soil, through roots, and into clouds — then falls as rain to start again. The water in your glass might have once been a dinosaur's river.

Try It!
1
Look at one seed through your magnifying glass. Can you see the tiny lines? Those are channels where water will FLOW once you plant it.
2
Hold the seed in your palm and breathe warm air on it. You just gave it the first two things it needs — warmth and moisture — to start flowing into life.
3
Plant your seed at home. Watch where the water goes when you pour it. Does it flow straight down? Does it spread? That is FLOW — happening right in your pot.
