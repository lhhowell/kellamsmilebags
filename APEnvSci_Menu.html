🌿 The Unit
AP Environmental Science students connect the real-world environmental science behind Operation Smiles — the causes of cleft conditions, 3D printing filament lifecycle, and acrylic mirror materials analysis — to a hands-on service contribution. Students build three original items that travel into Smile Bags for middle-school recipients: DNA Trait Cards, Smile Mirror Stickers, and Research Bookmarks. Future Ready attributes: Thinkers & Inquirers and Personally & Socially Responsible.
