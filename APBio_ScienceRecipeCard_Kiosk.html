⏰ Time
5 minutes today to get started
Check every day! Watch what happens
📄 Your steps
1
Find a sunny spot near a window at home — that will be your science station!
2
Place your seeds in a damp paper towel or a small cup of soil. You can even use a plastic bag taped to the window.
3
In your Mini Notepad, write today’s date and draw what your seeds look like right now.
4
Check every day! Use your Ruler to measure any changes. Draw what you see in your notepad.
👀 Watch for
A tiny white root peeking out first — then a green shoot reaching toward the light. That’s your seed deciding it’s time to GROW!
🧬 The science secret
Seeds know when conditions are right to grow — warmth, water, and light send signals to the seed to wake up. Scientists call this germination. You are watching biology happen in real time!
