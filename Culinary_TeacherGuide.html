3D Printing
Managing the Bambu Print Queue

After students submit their orders, open the KSB Cookie Cutter Orders spreadsheet. Sort by the Color column to batch prints by filament — this minimizes filament swaps on the Bambu Lab.

  • 1
    Open the spreadsheet
    Filter by color to see all Red orders, all Blue orders, etc.
  • 2
    Check cutter type
    Elementary & Middle School = Image Shape (thematic). High School = Word Shape (text cookie cutter).
  • 3
    Print each color batch
    Load filament → print all orders for that color → swap filament → next batch.
  • 4
    Add Play-Doh
    Each printed cutter ships with a mini Play-Doh container in the Smile Bag.
In the Classroom
Running the Kiosk
  • 1
    Share the hub URL
    Post lhhowell.github.io/kellamsmilebags/Culinary_Menu.html in Canvas or project on screen.
  • 2
    All students complete Option A first
    Cookie Cutter Order — this populates your Bambu print queue.
  • 3
    Students choose Option B, C, or D
    Choice activity — print their card when done.
  • 4
    After class — check spreadsheets
    Cookie Cutter Orders for print queue. Culinary Activities for B/C/D submissions.
  • 5
    Assemble bags
    Printed cutter + Play-Doh + student's activity card go into each Smile Bag.
