Design Your Smile Mirror Sticker 🪞

You'll create a 2.8″ circular adhesive sticker that goes on the back of an acrylic mirror in a Smile Bag. A child will peel it, stick it, and smile every time they see it.

🎨 Your design will be printed on adhesive sticker paper and cut to a 2.8″ circle by the Cricut. Think circular, bold, and uplifting!
