How to Use This Menu

Browse all four categories below. Select 10 items total spanning at least 3 categories. Items marked 🏦 Zoo Project connect directly to your AP Bio fieldwork. Items marked with a dashed border are student choice — sponsor approved. All items must be designed for a K–5 elementary student — keep language simple, visuals engaging, and activities completable with crayons or a pencil.

📌 All supplies and materials listed are provided through the project. Any materials not listed are the student's responsibility.  View full supply list →

Binary Coding Activity
Design a binary coding sheet where the elementary student decodes a hidden message, an animal name, or your OneWord using 0s and 1s. Include a simple key and clear instructions. Connect to the biology fact that DNA is nature's binary code — four bases, infinite combinations.
Coding & DecodingInteractive5×7 · B&W or color

Secret Cipher Sheet
Design an original cipher where each letter maps to a symbol, animal, or nature image. The student decodes a hidden message connected to your OneWord or an animal. Include the cipher key and a fun decoded message waiting to be found. Kid-friendly and completable with a pencil.
Coding & DecodingInteractive5×7 · B&W
8
Color-by-Code Sheet
Design a color-by-number or color-by-code sheet where solving the code reveals a hidden animal, ecosystem scene, or your OneWord illustrated in color. Numbers or symbols correspond to colors. The student colors it in with the crayons from the supply bar.
Coding & Decoding5×7 or larger · B&W to color
9
Decode the OneWord
Design a multi-step decoding activity where the elementary student works through 2–3 simple puzzles (word scramble, picture clue, pattern) to reveal your OneWord at the end. Include a brief explanation of what the word means in science and in everyday life — written for a child.
Coding & Decoding5×7 · front and back

Student Choice — Coding & Decoding
