🔬 AP Biology · Trophic Cascade
Lions are apex predators that control the entire food web below them. When lions are removed from an ecosystem, herbivore populations explode, vegetation is overgrazed, and rivers can even change course — this is called a trophic cascade. Every organism in a food web depends on balance at the top.
