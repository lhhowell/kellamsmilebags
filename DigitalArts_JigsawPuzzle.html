🎨 Color each piece with the brush, fill, or eraser tools — choose colors and brush size to taste.
✂️ Design your own cut lines! Click the scissors tool, then draw curves, zig-zags, or jigsaw shapes wherever you want pieces to separate. Your cut lines are your puzzle's signature.
📐 Piece count should match your recipient's age — a 20-piece puzzle for a 5-year-old is a design failure.
🪚 After printing, laminate, then cut along your drawn lines.
