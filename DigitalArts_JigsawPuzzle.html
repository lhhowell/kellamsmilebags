Step 3 · Piece Count
Match the puzzle to the child
📐 Piece count should match the recipient's age — a 24-piece puzzle for a 4-year-old is a design failure. Think about who will open this bag.

This is exactly what your cut template will look like — classic interlocking jigsaw pieces drawn as a transparent overlay on the grayscale illustration. The PDF is ready for Cricut, Scan N Cut, or laser cutter. The recipient will receive a precut puzzle and can color it whenever they're ready.
