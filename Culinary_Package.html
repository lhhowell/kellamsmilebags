The Four Options
What Students Do
Option A is required for all students. Options B, C, and D are choice activities.
A
🖨️ Cookie Cutter Order — REQUIRED
Culinary_Kiosk_v3.html
Student picks OneWord → recipient level → filament color → submits order. You print on Bambu Lab. Every student gets one 3D printed cookie cutter in their bag.
B
📖 OneWord Recipe Card — Choice
Culinary_RecipeCard.html
Student picks OneWord → chooses a recipe connected to its meaning → adds personal message → prints card for the bag.
C
🧬 DNA Flavor Pairing Lab — Choice
Culinary_DNAFlavor.html
Molecular gastronomy — 4 experiment modes. Student discovers flavor compounds connecting unexpected foods, prints results card.
D
💻 Bake the Code — Choice
Culinary_BakeTheCode.html
Binary decoder → unlock cookie decoration instructions → free-paint canvas → print decoration card.
For Students
Student Workflow
  • 1
    Open the Kiosk Hub — lhhowell.github.io/kellamsmilebags/Culinary_Menu.html
  • 2
    Complete Option A — Cookie Cutter Order. Choose your OneWord, recipient level, and filament color. Submit your order.
  • 3
    Choose Option B, C, or D — Pick the activity that interests you most and complete it.
  • 4
    Print your card — Use the print button inside the kiosk. Hand it to your teacher with your submission code.
Teacher Setup
Before You Launch
  • 1
    Confirm Apps Script URLs — Both kiosks submit to Google Sheets via Apps Script. Verify both spreadsheets are receiving data before running with students.
  • 2
    Post the hub URL in Canvas — lhhowell.github.io/kellamsmilebags/Culinary_Menu.html
  • 3
    Load Bambu Lab filament — 7 colors available. Have all colors ready or note which are unavailable.
  • 4
    Stock Play-Doh — One mini container per student goes in the bag alongside the cookie cutter.
  • 5
    After class — Open the Cookie Cutter Orders spreadsheet, sort by color, batch your Bambu prints.
