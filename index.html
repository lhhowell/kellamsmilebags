Smile Bags.

A cross-curricular service-learning project connecting Kellam students to elementary, middle, and high school students in our community who need a smile.

Classes16 Participating
Student Contributions10 Unique Items
LaunchMarch 2026
Shared Word25 OneWords
Clubs2 Participating
🌍

Each smile bag contains items handmade by Kellam students — one item per class, one word connecting them all. The 25 OneWord vocabulary words each carry two meanings — a science meaning rooted in VBCPS Environmental Science standards, and a human meaning grounded in social-emotional awareness, written directly for a child who is healing.

Participating Classes

Who's Building the Bags

Each participating class contributes at least one student-made item to every smile bag. A sneak preview of select materials is available below.

Smile Bag Guidelines

All smile bag items must be student-made. The only acceptable purchased items are crayons, colored pencils, paint kits, and Play-Doh.

✦ New items will be added as we bring on more participants.

● AVID

OneWord Inspirational Quote Card

Deena Vinson · AVID · Grades 9–12 · Costa's Levels of Thinking · Quick Write · Gemini Writing Tutor

AVID students apply Costa's Levels of Thinking and the Quick Write strategy to craft an original 120-character inspirational quote connected to one of the 25 shared OneWords. Students work with a Gemini AI writing tutor to deepen their thinking through Socratic questioning before designing a 4-inch square or circle quote card in their chosen Kellam color pair and font. The student's name, OneWord, and quote are printed on cardstock and placed in each Smile Bag. Future Ready: Balanced Communicators & Collaborators — writing with purpose for a real audience; Personally & Socially Responsible — applying AVID strategies in direct service of a child who needs encouragement.

💬 Contribution: Original inspirational quote card · 4″ square or circle · printed on cardstock
