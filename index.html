Smile Bags.

A cross-curricular service-learning project connecting Kellam students to elementary, middle, and high school students in our community who need a smile.

Each smile bag contains items handmade by Kellam students — one item per class, one word connecting them all. The child who receives a BLOOM bookmark also receives the BLOOM coloring book page, written for them by a Kellam student. The 25 OneWord vocabulary words were selected because each carries two meanings — a science meaning rooted in VBCPS Environmental Science standards, and a human meaning grounded in social-emotional awareness, written directly for a child who is healing.

Participating Classes

Who's Building the Bags

Each participating class and club contributes at least one student-made item to every smile bag — some contribute more. Document packages will be available for viewing after the project is completed in Spring 2026. A sneak preview of select materials is already available for the Kellam Smile Bags Mascot Sticker & Hidden Picture Activities and the Did You Know? Research Bookmark cards below.

Smile Bag Guidelines

All smile bag items must be student-made. The only acceptable purchased items are crayons, colored pencils, paint kits, and Play-Doh — these may be included to accompany the student-made coloring book page.

✦ New items will be added as we bring on more participants.

● AP Research

Did You Know? Research Bookmark

Abbie McGee · AP Research course framework · Science Supply Bar · Bookmark Builder Kiosk

AP Research students translate a year of research thinking into a ‘Did You Know?’ bookmark for a student in our community. Each student selects their OneWord, browses the Science Supply Bar — choosing 1–3 hands-on supplies based on their own scientific reasoning — and builds their bookmark using the digital Bookmark Builder Kiosk. Future Ready attributes: Thinkers & Inquirers — selecting tools based on scientific hypothesis; Personally & Socially Responsible — applying research expertise in service of a student who needs a smile.
