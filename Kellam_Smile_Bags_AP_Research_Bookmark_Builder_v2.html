How to use this kiosk:
1. Click your assigned OneWord on the left — your bookmark pre-populates with a research fact, activity, and message.
2. Read through the content. Edit any field to make it your own — your words, your voice.
3. When you are happy with your bookmark, click Print Bookmark to print on cardstock, or Save as PDF to save a digital copy.
4. Print front and back on the same sheet of cardstock · Laminate for durability · Punch a hole at the top for a ribbon.
Tip: The bookmark is 2 inches × 7 inches. Print at actual size (100% scale) for best results.
