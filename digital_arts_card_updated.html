● Digital Arts

AI Art Styles · Stencil Set · Jigsaw Puzzle · Signature Sticker Design

Kara Gau · Virginia Visual Arts SOL AI.1 · AI.2 · AII.7 · AII.10

Digital Arts students explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and visual design through three interconnected contributions. Students study AI-generated art styles across the OneWord Coloring Book — comparing ChatGPT, CoPilot, and Gemini outputs across scenic, artistic, woodcut, cartoon, K–4 icon, nature poster, and fine line botanical styles — then download and print pages for the bags. Students design stencil sets for each OneWord: the word itself plus 2–3 symbolic images for canvas painting. Students also create jigsaw puzzles using the Set G nature poster images — printed, laminated, and cut into 8–20 pieces. The project framework centers on empathy-driven design: every creative decision is made with a specific child recipient in mind. Future Ready: Problem Solvers & Value Creators; Personally & Socially Responsible.
