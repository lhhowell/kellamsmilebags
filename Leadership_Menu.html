At Kellam High School, Leadership students are building complete, personalized activity packages for children receiving Smile Bags. Each package contains five leadership-building activities plus a personal handwritten letter guided by a Gemini AI writing tutor — all connected to one shared word chosen with intention.

At the heart of Smile Bags are 25 OneWords — connecting environmental science to social emotional well-being.

Each of the 25 OneWords lives in two worlds at once — in the natural systems students study, and in the inner lives students navigate every day. From Absorb to Transform, these words remind us that the same forces that sustain ecosystems are the forces that sustain people too.

Who Receives a Smile Bag

A bag finds its way to a student
who needs it most.

Smile Bags are distributed across Kellam's eight feeder schools and Kellam High School itself — ten bags per school, ninety students reached. Guidance counselors identify students who may need encouragement, motivation, or simply a reason to smile. A bag is chosen for them deliberately, not randomly — at exactly the right moment.

Every item inside was made by a Kellam student who didn't know your name — but made something just for you.

The Package
6 Activities, 1 OneWord
Tab 01
🧩 Puzzle It Out
A riddle connected to the student's OneWord, complete with an answer and a lesson. Students write their own original version tied to their word's meaning.
Tab 02
💛 Kindness Coupons
Four coupons for genuine acts of service. Students write the specific acts — personalized, meaningful, and cut-ready for the recipient to redeem.
Tab 03
🌬️ Calm-Down Toolkit
A breathing technique named after the student's OneWord. Includes an interactive breathing circle that demonstrates the technique in real time.
Tab 04
✨ Dream Jar
A fill-in activity guiding a child through naming a dream, setting a goal, and writing a message to themselves. Age-calibrated prompts for each level.
Tab 05
💡 Big Idea Planner
A structured planning sheet with steps, resources, and a purpose statement. 3–5 steps depending on age group. Leadership thinking for a child just starting out.
Tab 06 · Special
✉️ Handwritten Letter
Student writes a personal letter guided by a Gemini AI writing tutor. Submitted for teacher approval before printing. Written by hand on cardstock and placed in the bag.
