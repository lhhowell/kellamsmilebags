💕 The T.I.D.E. Connection
T.I.D.E. — Togetherness through Inclusion, Diversity, and Equity — is a student-led initiative in Virginia Beach City Public Schools. T.I.D.E. students believe every person deserves to feel like they belong, exactly as they are. This card was made by a Kellam High School student who wanted you to know: you matter, you belong, and someone who has never met you made something just for you.
