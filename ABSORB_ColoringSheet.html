ABSORB — Vocabulary Coloring Sheet
Kellam Smile Bags · Kellam High School · Spring 2026
BUTTERFLY
A winged insect that floats from flower to flower, drinking in nectar and spreading life.
SPIDER WEB
A delicate silk structure that catches dewdrops, light, and everything that drifts by.
LADYBUG
A tiny spotted beetle that absorbs sunlight on leaves and helps gardens grow.
RAINBOW
Light absorbed by water droplets and released as a spectrum of beautiful color.
BIRD'S NEST
A carefully built home that absorbs warmth and protects new life growing inside.
SNAIL
A slow, mindful creature that absorbs its surroundings one gentle step at a time.
DRAGONFLY
An ancient insect that hovers near water, absorbing the sights and sounds around it.
ANT
A tiny but mighty creature that absorbs its environment through touch, smell, and teamwork.
DEWDROP
A tiny drop of water that forms overnight by absorbing moisture from the cool night air.
MUSHROOM
A fungi that absorbs nutrients from the forest floor, quietly recycling the earth.
