ABSORB

🎁 Gratitude
  1. Write a thank-you note to a teacher who explained something hard so well it stuck
  2. Tell the librarian what your favorite book taught you
  3. Listen carefully to a friend's story, then tell them what you loved about it
🗺️ Adventure
  1. Visit the school library and pick a book about a place you've never been
  2. Read about an animal that lives somewhere far away — share one fact with a friend
  3. Watch a nature video and learn 3 brand-new facts
🌈 Happiness/Memory
  1. Draw the happiest moment from your school week
  2. Write down one new word you learned today so you remember it forever
  3. Tell a friend about a class you really enjoyed this week

ADAPT

🎁 Gratitude
  1. Thank someone who helps you when plans change at school
  2. Write a quick note to a teacher who tries new ways to teach
  3. Tell a friend you appreciate how they roll with surprises
🗺️ Adventure
  1. Try walking a brand-new path from your classroom to the cafeteria
  2. Read a book set in a different country and learn one custom
  3. Imagine you live in a tree house — draw a whole day in your tree
🌈 Happiness/Memory
  1. Remember a time you tried something new at school and it went great — draw it
  2. Write down a happy surprise that happened this week
  3. Tell a friend about a time you made the best of a change

ANCHOR

🗺️ Adventure

  3. Imagine your dream cozy reading nook — draw every detail
🌈 Happiness/Memory
  1. Draw your favorite spot at school that always feels safe and steady
  2. Write about a day at school you felt totally settled and ready
  3. List 3 places at school where you feel calm and ready to learn

BLOOM

🌈 Happiness/Memory
  1. Draw the most colorful flower you've ever seen
  2. Remember a time at school you bloomed into something new — write it down
  3. Make a happy memory page about your favorite spring day

🌈 Happiness/Memory

  3. Take 5 slow breaths and picture your happiest school day
