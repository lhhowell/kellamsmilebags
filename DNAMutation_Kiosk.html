DNA Base Pair Encoding
Each letter of the OneWord is encoded as a pair of DNA bases (A-T or G-C). One base in the strand has been deliberately mutated to a wrong base — changing one letter of the hidden word. The middle school student must find the mutation, correct it, and decode the true message.
