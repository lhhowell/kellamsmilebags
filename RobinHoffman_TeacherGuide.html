Live Kiosks
Ready-to-Use Student Tools
🍫
Candy Bar Wrap Designer
lhhowell.github.io/kellamsmilebags/RobinHoffman_CandyBarKiosk.html
Students enter their name, choose a OneWord, write an original 100-character inspirational message, select a color theme, preview the wrap, and print. The wrap fits a standard 1.55oz Hershey bar. Logs to Google Sheets when deployed.
🏷️
Custom Sticker Sheet Designer
lhhowell.github.io/kellamsmilebags/RobinHoffman_StickerSheet.html
Students enter their name, choose a OneWord, pick a sticker style (Classic, Botanical, Badge, or Minimal), and print a full sheet of 12 personalized stickers. Works best on sticker paper (Avery 22807 round labels) but regular paper works too.

In the Classroom
Suggested Workflow
  • 1
    Review the Choice Menu
    Open RobinHoffman_ChoiceMenu.html and tap 💡 See Example on any item to preview a sample student output. Select which items each class will complete. Share your selections with Ms. Howell.
  • 2
    Share the Hub URL
    Post lhhowell.github.io/kellamsmilebags/RobinHoffman_Menu.html in Canvas or display on screen as students start working.
  • 3
    Kiosk Activities
    Direct students to the Candy Bar Wrap Kiosk or Sticker Sheet Designer. Both have step-by-step screens and print directly from the browser.
  • 4
    Print-Based Activities
    Use the print templates Ms. Howell provides for your selected items. Students complete them on paper or digitally, then print.
  • 5
    Quality Review
    Review all items for age-appropriateness and completeness before assembly. Bags go to Kellam students — content should be warm, genuine, and encouraging.
  • 6
    Assemble & Deliver by May 30
    Completed items go to Ms. Howell for final bag assembly and delivery to Kellam guidance counselors.
