OneWord Recipe Card Kiosk
Students select a OneWord, generate a themed recipe, cook and taste, then rate and reflect.

Learning Objective
Students will select a OneWord from the Smile Bags vocabulary list, use a digital kiosk to generate a themed recipe connected to that word's meaning, prepare and taste the recipe in a professional culinary setting, and evaluate the result using a structured star rating and written reflection — contributing their feedback to a class-wide data set.
📋
Future Ready Virginia Beach Standards
FR · CTE
Career & Technical Education: Students apply industry-standard culinary techniques in a real-world service-learning context, producing items intended for community distribution.
FR · CC
Critical Thinking & Communication: Students evaluate recipe outcomes using structured criteria, articulate observations in written form, and connect culinary science to vocabulary meaning.
FR · SR
Socially Responsible: Students contribute to a cross-curricular service-learning project that directly benefits elementary school students in need of encouragement and support.
FR · DL
Digital Literacy: Students navigate a web-based kiosk to select, generate, and submit a recipe card — using technology as a purposeful creative and organizational tool.

90-Minute Lesson Timeline
Block Schedule
0:00
10 min
Introduction & Kiosk Demo
Teacher introduces the Smile Bags project context and models navigating the OneWord Recipe Card Kiosk on the projector. Students review the 25 OneWords and begin thinking about which word resonates with them.
0:10
15 min
Kiosk Navigation & Recipe Selection
Students open the kiosk, enter their name and class, select their OneWord, and choose one of two recipe options. They review the recipe card — ingredients, steps, and the "Why this word?" connection — and submit their card to the class log.
0:25
10 min
Mise en Place & Lab Prep
Students wash hands, gather equipment, and measure/organize ingredients based on their recipe card. Teacher reviews key techniques and safety reminders relevant to the day's recipes.
0:35
35 min
Cooking Lab
Students prepare their recipe following the kiosk-generated steps. Teacher circulates to support technique, safety, and quality. Students practice professional kitchen behaviors appropriate to their class level.
  • Intro to Culinary: focus on foundational techniques and following instructions precisely
  • Catering: focus on production efficiency, plating, and presentation quality
1:10
10 min
Taste & Evaluate
Students taste their completed dish and return to the kiosk to submit a 1–5 star rating and written comment. Ratings log automatically to the class spreadsheet for Ms. Sayers to review.
1:20
10 min
Clean Up & Reflection
Students clean and sanitize their stations to industry standards. Class closes with a brief discussion: Which words got the highest ratings? What would you change about the recipe? How does the recipe connect to the word's meaning?
📊
Assessment
🖥️ Kiosk Submission
Completion of the recipe card kiosk — name, word selection, recipe choice, and dedication field submitted to the class log.
🍳 Lab Performance
Teacher observation of technique, safety, mise en place, and professional kitchen behavior during the cooking lab.
⭐ Taste Rating
Completion of 1–5 star rating and written comment submitted through the kiosk after tasting — logged to the class spreadsheet.
🧹 Station Sanitation
End-of-lab station check — equipment cleaned, surfaces sanitized, and materials returned to appropriate storage.
🌱
Differentiation
🤝 Support
Pre-selected word option available. Teacher pairs student with a lab partner. Printed recipe card provided as a physical backup. Step-by-step technique check-ins during lab.
✅ On Level
Student selects their own word and recipe independently. Follows kiosk steps and lab procedures with standard teacher support. Completes rating and reflection.
🚀 Extension
Student modifies the recipe with one ingredient substitution and notes the change. Writes an extended reflection connecting the recipe to the word's deeper meaning. Presents to class.
💙
Smile Bags Connection
The recipe card produced in this lesson represents Culinary Arts' contribution to the Smile Bags project — a cross-curricular service-learning initiative serving elementary students at Three Oaks Elementary. Students' recipe cards, once refined through the rating process, will be printed on cardstock and placed inside Smile Bags as a gift for a family to enjoy together. Every recipe is an act of care for a child who needs it.
