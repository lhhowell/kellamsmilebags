How it works
Cut, scramble, and reassemble
Choose your OneWord. The kiosk generates a short 4-panel story connected to your word, printed in scrambled order. The student receiving the bag cuts the panels apart, reads each one, and puts the story back in the right sequence. Answer boxes at the bottom let them record the correct order. The story reveal and science connection are printed on the back of the sheet.
