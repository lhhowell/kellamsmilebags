How the Science Supply Bar Works

Browse the supplies below. Choose 1–3 items that connect to your OneWord and your research. In your bookmark, you will explain why you chose each item using your scientific reasoning. Your supplies accompany your “Did You Know?” bookmark in the Smile Bag — giving a middle school student something to explore and discover!

Each supply is chosen to connect to real science topics that middle school students study. When you write your bookmark, explain which supply you chose and exactly why it connects to your OneWord and your research. The more specific your scientific reasoning, the stronger your bookmark!

Not sure which to choose? Ask yourself: "If I gave this to a 6th, 7th, or 8th grader and explained the science behind it, what would they learn?"
