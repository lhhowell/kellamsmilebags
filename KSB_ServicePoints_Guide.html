What is a Smile Bag?

Every child deserves to feel seen. A Smile Bag is a handmade gift bag filled with at least 10 original items — bookmarks, spinners, stickers, quote cards, and more — each one personally crafted by a Kellam student. These bags are delivered to children in our community who are facing hardship, illness, or uncertainty. Your creativity becomes someone else's hope. Build a complete bag through your club and earn service points!

🆕

Club Members Only — Service Points Through Your Sponsor

Service points for Kellam Smile Bags are awarded exclusively through club sponsors. You must be participating as a club member and have your club sponsor verify your completed bag before points are awarded. Not sure if your club is participating? Ask your sponsor!

📄 How to Earn Your Service Points
1

Print & get your permission slip signed

Print the permission slip from this page, have your club sponsor sign it, and return it to Ms. Howell in D210 before you begin. Your sponsor only confirms you are an active club member — nothing else is required from them.

2

Visit the kiosks and create your items

Use any of the kiosks listed below to design and submit items. You need items from at least 4 different activities. Every submission gives you a KSB code — write it in your tracker!

3

Reach 10 items total

You can submit multiple items from the same kiosk, but you need a minimum of 10 items total from at least 4 different activities to qualify for a complete bag.

4

Check the box & submit when you are ready

Once you have completed your 10 items, check the confirmation box at the bottom of this page and submit. Ms. Howell will verify your KSB codes and notify your sponsor directly — no further action is needed from you or your sponsor. Upon verification, 5 service points will be awarded and your bag will be assembled and delivered!

⚠️ Important: Every item must be submitted through a kiosk with a KSB code (KSB-XXXX). Items without a code cannot be counted. You may earn 5 service points for one complete bag per semester. Ms. Howell verifies all submission codes — your sponsor only needs to confirm you have club permission.
