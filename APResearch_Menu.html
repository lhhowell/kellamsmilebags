📚 The Unit
AP Research students translate research thinking into a hands-on contribution: a Science Bookmark built around a OneWord, paired with a set of supplies selected from the Science Supply Bar. Each completed bag travels to an elementary recipient with a fact, an activity, and a personal message from a high school scientist. Future Ready attributes: Thinkers & Inquirers and Personally & Socially Responsible.
